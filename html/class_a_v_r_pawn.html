◆ UpdateHardwareTrackingState()

void AVRPawn::UpdateHardwareTrackingState ( )
protected

Disable/Enable collisions on the whole pawn including hands individually from each other based on current tracking status.... NOTE: When this is not enabled, when the device loses tracking and repositions itself when found again the sweep will cause physic actors in the scene to be affected by the force of movement... NOTE: Could also use SetWorldLocation/Rotation no physics functions but I have no control over the object being spawned and positioned on begin play.
